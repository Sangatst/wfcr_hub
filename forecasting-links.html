Forecasting Links

Access to external weather forecasting resources and websites

The Center analyzes from open and access-allowed to national meteorological and hydrological service providers' hydrometeorological websites. The forecasters on duty shall input the information from the following websites. These websites will be the base platforms and forecast guidance is not limited to these sites.

  • India Meteorological Department
  • Indian Monsoon data Regional output
  • National Center for Medium-Range Weather Forecasting
  • Short Range Ensemble Prediction System
  • Indian Institute of Tropical Meteorology
  • Severe Weather Forecast Project
  • Regional Specialized Meteorological Center for Tropical Cyclones Over North Indian Ocean, IMD
  • Thai Meteorological Department
  • China Meteorological Administration
  • European Center for Medium-Range Weather Forecast
  • MetGIS
  • Meteoblue
  • Windy.com
  • South Asian Flash Flood Guidance
  • Tropical Tidbits
  • Earth Null School
