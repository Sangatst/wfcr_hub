Weather Services

The National Center for Hydrology and Meteorology (NCHM) provides a comprehensive range of weather services tailored to meet the needs of various sectors and the general public. Our services combine scientific expertise with advanced technology to deliver accurate and timely weather information.

Back to Dashboard

Public Weather Services

Daily forecasts and weather information for the general public

Our Public Weather Services provide essential weather information to the general public, government agencies, and various sectors. These services are designed to be accessible, easy to understand, and relevant to daily decision-making.

NCHM delivers weather forecasts through multiple channels including our website, mobile applications, social media, radio, television, and SMS alerts. Our forecasts cover temperature, precipitation, wind, humidity, and other weather parameters for all districts and major cities in Bhutan.

Daily Forecasts

24-hour and 5-day forecasts for all districts and major cities, updated four times daily.

Weather Warnings

Timely alerts for severe weather events including heavy rainfall, thunderstorms, and extreme temperatures.

Weather Maps

Visual representations of current and forecast weather conditions across the country.

Weather News

Regular updates on significant weather events and their potential impacts.

Aviation Weather

Specialized forecasts for aviation operations

NCHM provides specialized aviation weather services to support safe and efficient air operations in Bhutan's challenging mountainous terrain. Our aviation forecasts adhere to International Civil Aviation Organization (ICAO) standards and are tailored to the specific needs of pilots, air traffic controllers, and airport operators.

Given Bhutan's unique topography, our aviation weather services incorporate detailed knowledge of local weather patterns and terrain-induced phenomena to enhance flight safety in the region.

Terminal Aerodrome Forecasts (TAF)

Detailed forecasts for airports including wind, visibility, cloud, and weather conditions.

SIGMET & AIRMET

Information about weather phenomena that may affect aircraft safety, including turbulence, icing, and mountain waves.

Route Forecasts

Weather information for specific flight routes, including wind and temperature at various flight levels.

Pre-flight Briefings

Comprehensive weather briefings for pilots before departure, highlighting potential hazards.

Agricultural Weather

Weather information tailored for agricultural planning and operations

Our Agricultural Weather Services provide farmers, agricultural extension officers, and policymakers with specialized weather information to support agricultural planning and operations. These services help optimize planting and harvesting schedules, manage irrigation, and mitigate weather-related risks to crops and livestock.

NCHM collaborates closely with the Ministry of Agriculture and Forests to ensure that our agricultural weather services address the specific needs of Bhutan's diverse agricultural systems across different agro-ecological zones.

Agro-meteorological Bulletins

Weekly bulletins with weather information relevant to agricultural activities and crop-specific advisories.

Seasonal Outlooks

Long-term forecasts to support seasonal planning for planting, irrigation, and harvesting.

Frost and Freeze Warnings

Early warnings for frost events to help farmers protect sensitive crops.

Crop Weather Calendars

Customized calendars that align weather patterns with optimal timing for various agricultural activities.

Hydrometeorological Services

Water-related weather services for flood forecasting and water management

NCHM's Hydrometeorological Services focus on the interaction between weather and water resources. These services are crucial for flood forecasting, water resource management, hydropower operations, and disaster risk reduction in Bhutan's river basins.

Our hydrometeorological monitoring network includes automated weather stations, river gauges, and snow monitoring sites that provide real-time data on precipitation, river levels, and snowpack conditions. This data feeds into our hydrological models to generate forecasts and warnings.

Flood Forecasting

Early warning system for potential floods based on rainfall forecasts and river basin conditions.

Glacial Lake Outburst Flood (GLOF) Monitoring

Specialized monitoring and early warning for glacial lake outburst floods in high mountain areas.

Drought Monitoring

Regular assessment of drought conditions and their potential impacts on water resources.

Snowpack Analysis

Monitoring and forecasting of snow accumulation and melt for water resource planning and avalanche risk assessment.

Time Series 10 Days Forecast

Detailed weather forecasts with time series data

Our Time Series 10 Days Forecast service provides detailed weather predictions for various locations across Bhutan. This service includes comprehensive time series data for temperature, precipitation, wind speed, and other meteorological parameters.

The forecasts are generated using advanced numerical weather prediction models and are updated daily to ensure accuracy and reliability.

Temperature Forecasts

Hourly and daily temperature predictions for the next 10 days.

Precipitation Forecasts

Detailed rainfall and snowfall predictions with probability estimates.

Wind Forecasts

Wind speed and direction predictions for various locations.

Weather Parameters

Additional parameters including humidity, pressure, and visibility.

3 Days Map Forecast

Visual weather maps for the next 3 days

The 3 Days Map Forecast service provides visual representations of weather conditions across Bhutan. These maps help users understand weather patterns and make informed decisions based on forecasted conditions.

Our maps are generated using high-resolution weather models and are updated regularly to reflect the latest forecast data.

Temperature Maps

Color-coded maps showing temperature variations across regions.

Precipitation Maps

Rainfall and snowfall distribution maps with intensity indicators.

Wind Maps

Wind flow patterns and speed distribution across the country.

Weather Systems

Maps showing weather systems, fronts, and pressure patterns.

Warning Maps

Real-time warning maps for severe weather conditions

Our Warning Maps service provides real-time information about severe weather conditions across Bhutan. These maps are crucial for emergency response and public safety, particularly during extreme weather events.

The warning system includes both wind and rainfall warnings, with different severity levels to help users understand the potential impact of weather conditions.

Wind Warning Maps

Real-time maps showing areas affected by strong winds and gusts.

Rainfall Warning Maps

Maps indicating areas with heavy rainfall and potential flooding risks.

Warning Levels

Color-coded warning levels indicating severity of weather conditions.

Impact Assessment

Information about potential impacts of severe weather conditions.

Smartmet Products

Advanced weather products powered by Smartmet technology

The Smartmet Products service offers advanced weather forecasting tools and products powered by state-of-the-art Smartmet technology. These products provide detailed and accurate weather information for various applications.

Our Smartmet products are designed to meet the needs of different users, from meteorologists to the general public, with user-friendly interfaces and comprehensive data visualization.

Forecast Products

Advanced forecasting tools with high-resolution data and visualization.

Analysis Tools

Tools for analyzing weather patterns and historical data.

Custom Solutions

Tailored weather products for specific user requirements.

Data Integration

Tools for integrating weather data with other systems and applications.
